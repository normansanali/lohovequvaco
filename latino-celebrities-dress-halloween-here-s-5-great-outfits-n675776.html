Latino Celebrities Dress Up for Halloween: Here's 5 Great Outfits

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Happy Halloween! Here's a few of our favorite 2016 Halloween costumes from our Latino celebrities.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BMO7PHUAb4G

Similar to one of his songs titled La Bella y La Bestia (The Beauty and The Beast) the King of Bachata Romeo Santos dresses up as the beast this Halloween season.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BMPBNDVAZxv

The children of Jennifer Lopez and Marc Anthony are ready to celebrate Halloween with their adorable costumes, one like a little "gatita" (cat) and Bowser from the Mario games.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BMOpp7djW4A

Venezuelan comedian Daniel Sarcos is channeling acclaimed Chilean television host Don Francisco, a costume that has many Latinos on social media hysterically laughing.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BMO48Odhuwo

The first Dominican beauty pageant to be crowned by Nuestra Belleza Latina, Francisca LaChapel, celebrates Halloween as Pocahontas.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BMO3jJOjvB_

The Puerto Rican host of the Telemundo show Un Nuevo Dia, Adamari López, dresses up as the Puerto Rican rapper Daddy Yankee.
