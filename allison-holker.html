Allison Holker Career

Allison performed with Earth, Wind, and Fire in both the opening and closing ceremonies of the 2002 Winter Olympics. She went on to win titles in numerous dance events after the Olympics. In 2004, she won her first national competition in Co DANCE, and in 2005, she was named National Senior Outstanding Dancer by the New York City Dance Alliance.

She has also appeared in Disney’s High School Musical and High School Musical 2 films. She appeared in the second season of So You Think You Can Dance in 2006 after participating in the High School Musical. She finished eighth that season and also took part in the SYTYCD countrywide tour. She subsequently went on to appear in Louis Van Amstel’s choreographed production “Ballroom with a Twist.”
