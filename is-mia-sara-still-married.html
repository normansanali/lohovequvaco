Is Mia Sara still married?

Publish date: 2024-08-25

American actress Mia Sarapochiello, better known by her stage name Mia Sara, was born on June 19, 1967. She made her acting debut in the 1985 fantasy film Legend as Princess Lili, and she got her big break in the 1986 comedy film Ferris Bueller’s Day Off as Sloane Peterson. She also got the Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role as Melissa Walker in the 1994 science fiction film Timecop.

Mia Sarapochiello was Sara’s birth name when she was born in Brooklyn Heights, Brooklyn, New York. Diana Sarapochiello, a stylist and photographer, and Jerome Sarapochiello, an artist and photographer, are her parents. The family is of Italian descent and follows the Roman Catholic faith. She went to Brooklyn’s St. Ann’s School.

In Ridley Scott’s 1985 fairy tale film Legend, Sara made her acting debut as Princess Lili alongside Tom Cruise. Assuming the part of Ferris Bueller’s better half, Sloane Peterson, in the 1986 blockbuster film Ferris Bueller’s Free day made her considerably more well-known.

Is Mia Sara still married?

In Walk 1996, Sara wedded Jason Connery, the child of Sean Connery, with whom she acted in Projectile to Beijing. In 2002, the couple got divorced.

Amelia Jane Henson was Sara’s daughter with Brian Henson in 2005. In 2010, Sara and Henson tied the knot.
