Kylie Jenner Takes Paris Fashion Week in Belted Little Black Dress

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Kylie Jenner’s latest Paris Fashion Week look is a mix between sleek office attire and BDSM lingerie.

Last night, the makeup mogul was photographed out in the French city wearing a little black dress with matching heeled leather boots. The dress featured long sleeves and a pencil skirt with a high leg slit, but the torso is where it got interesting. The front of of the piece was entirely cut out, with only three thick belts going across Jenner’s torso and under-bust holding it together.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder finished the dark, elevated look with a small black handbag, sheer black gloves, a glamorous side-parted ‘do, bronzy makeup, and a maroon lip.

MEGA

Jenner has been on a Wednesday-core style streak as of late. Earlier this month, she shared photos on Instagram of herself wearing a black strapless denim jumpsuit and sultry punk-chic glam: heavy eyeliner, a dramatic updo, and bleached brows.

In December, she stepped out for a night in Beverly Hills in a completely sheer black slipdress embroidered with floral lace details and showing off her black bra and underwear underneath. She styled the piece with an oversized leather biker jacket by David Koma, a black mini handbag, matching kitten heels, and silver hoop earrings.

Jenner similarly embraced the sheer LBD trend in November, when she wore a custom backless gown by Loewe—featuring a high halter neck that tied in a flouncy bow at the back and a micro-mini skirt with a sheer maxi-length overlay—to the Baby2Baby Gala. Sister Kim Kardashian received the charity's Giving Tree Award at the event.
