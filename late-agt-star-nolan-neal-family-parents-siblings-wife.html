Who Are Late AGT Star Nolan Neal Family Members?

Late AGT star Nolan Neal Seals grew up in a household filled with musicians. Both of his parents were musicians, and also his cousins. According to PEOPLE, his cousin Dylan Seals confirmed his death to the outlet. According to the outlet, Nolan “ultimately succumbed to his battle with substance abuse”.

The medical examiner confirmed to TMZ that he died on 18 July 2022. According to the outlet, he was found dead in his apartment. His other cousin, Dana Dylan also posted about Nolan’s death on her Facebook.

Learn more about his family members in this article below.

Meet Late AGT Star Nolan Neal Parents

Nolan Neal Seals from AGT was born to his father Mark Daniel Seals AKA Welby and mother Cathy Lee.

Speaking about Nolan’s parents, his father Mark was a member of the First Baptist Church of Elizabethton. He was a musician who played with several bands as a drummer.

Moreover, Mark had played back-up for Kenny Rogers, Lynn Anderson, and George Jones in concerts and was a studio musician in Nashville. Mark was also a member of the American Legion and the VFW. He served with the U. S. Army in Korea during the Vietnam Conflict.

Whereas Nolan’s mother, Cathy was also a singer and played in a band. Though she doesn’t play music anymore, she still dedicates her life to art working as a faux artist/painter. As for her day job, she has been retired since October 2017.

Nolan has talked about his father through music. He wrote a song Hand In Hand addressing his father’s death by suicide. Born on 15 October 1952, Nolan’s father Mark died on 26 September 2005 at the age of 52 in UT Hospital, Knoxville, TN. He was the son of  Margaret (Peggy) Daniels Seals, Morristown, and the late Robert Neal Seals.

At the time of his death, Mark was married to his wife Angela Shockley Seals.

Nolan’s mother Cathy turned 65 years old in September 2021. Her parents Catherine and Wayne Lee met on a blind date, got married one month later, and made it 61 years until her dad passed away in January 2017.

On Mother’s Day 2017, Nolan wrote to his mom on Facebook, “Just want you to know I love you. You’re an amazing Woman. Thank you, Mom, for all you’ve done for me, Caylin, Adrienne, Cadence, Mamaw Lee and so many other souls in this world who were blessed to receive your selfless love”.

Did Late AGT Star Nolan Neal Have Any Siblings?

Nolan Neal was the only son born to his parents. So, he didn’t have any siblings. However, his two cousins were like his two siblings as they grew up together and were known as “the three”.

Dana Dylan and Dylan seals were born to Pastor Robert “Brent” Seals and Patricia Vineyard Seals. They lost their father at the age of 68 on 1 July 2019. Brent was always proud of his nephew Nolan.

Dana is a Cannes film actress and singer-songwriter. She attended South College-Asheville where she studied Health Sciences / Nursing and studied Family Consumer Science and Art at East Tennessee State University. She worked at Niswonger Children’s Hospital.

On the other hand, Dylan Seals, Dana’s brother, is a two times Grammy nominated audio engineer and media producer.
