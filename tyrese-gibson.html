Tyrese Gibson, also known as Tyrese Darnell Gibson is an American actor and singer. As of 2023, Tyrese Gibson’s net worth is $6 million. He rose to fame by releasing his debut studio album “Tyrese” in 1998. The single titled “Sweet Lady” from his debuted album ranked 12th on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100.

He released the single “How You Gonna Act Like That” from the album titled “I Wanna Go There”, which got the seventh position on the Billboard Hot 100. He also released the album “Black Rose” in July 2015, gaining No. 1 on the Billboard 200. Some of his famous music videos are “Rain”, “My Way”, “Angel of Mine”, “What’chu Like”, “Amor Amor” and others.

He has made his appearance as a significant character titled “Joseph ‘Jody’ Summers” in the 2001 crime drama film “Baby Boy”. He appeared as “Roman Pearce” in the 2003 hit action film series “2 Fast 2 Furious” and as “Simon Stroud” in the 2022 action movie titled “Morbius”.

He was featured as “Darrell” in the 1996 American sitcom titled “Hangin’ with Mr. Cooper” and “Dante” in the 1997 American television sitcom titled “Martin”. He also served as a host for a music video show titled “MTV Jams” and “VJ” for the MTV channel in 1998.
