How much is Nick Foles salary? How much are the Chicago Bears paying Nick Foles?

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Nick Foles is an American football quarterback for the Chicago Bears of the National Football League (NFL).

Nicholas Edward Foles was born on January 20, 1989, in, Austin, Texas, the United States. He is the son of restaurateurs Larry and Melissa Foles. Nick Foles graduated from Westlake High School in 2007.

Nick Foles attended Michigan State for one year after he had initially committed to Arizona State. He ultimately transferred to the University of Arizona where he had to redshirt his 2008 freshman season.

Nick Foles worked as an online graduate student at Liberty University in 2018 and later earned his master’s degree in divinity. He was selected by the Philadelphia Eagles in the third round with the 88th overall pick in the 2012 NFL Draft.

Nick Foles is presently married to Tori Moore, who is the younger sister of former NFL tight end Evan Moore. The pair tied the knot in 2014 and are proud parents of a daughter, Lily James Foles (born in 2017) and a son, Duke Nathan Foles (born on June 19, 2020).

How much is Nick Foles salary?

Nick Foles receives an average annual salary of $8,000,000. He had signed a 3 year, $24,000,000 contract with the Chicago Bears, including a $8,000,000 signing bonus, $21,000,000 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $8,000,000.

In 2021, Nick Foles will receive a base salary of $4,000,000, while carrying a cap hit of $6,666,666 and a dead cap value of $14,333,334.
