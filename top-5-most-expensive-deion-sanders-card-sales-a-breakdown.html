Top 5 Most Expensive Deion Sanders Card Sales: A Breakdown

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Here we will be uncovering the top 5 most expensive card sales of Deion Sanders, an iconic multi-sport athlete. 


The Deion Sanders Rookie Card Allure


Deion Sanders' rookie cards have long been some of the most coveted within many collectors' portfolios, and their seemingly irrefutable desirability is further showcased with current prices. The excitement of a Deion rookie, especially those that have gone for four figures and up, can be likened to literally holding sports history in your hand. In what follows, explore the magic of Deion Sanders through his top 5 most expensive card sales. Follow along as we take a fascinating voyage into select portions of his storied tenure in America.


1. 1989 Score #246 Deion Sanders ($11,249)

His 1989 Score #246 Deion Sanders card is a great example of this period and his parallel baseball career. This card has also been a biggie with a recent sale of the highest price ever recorded for this rookie issue, hammering away at $11,249... in like new condition and featuring "primer time" Sanders turning on an Atlanta Falcons base ball cap. The rarity of the card and its captivating image, which appears to predict Sander's future as a duel sports superstar are what make this card so appealing.


2. 1989 Topps Traded 30T Deion Sanders $4,716

A 1989 Topps Traded #30T Deion Sanders card - with an action shot of a Falcons-era Sanders just waiting for a punt return to break loose - demonstrating that "Prime Time" energy. Sanders' dynamic on-field presence continues to make collectors do a double-take, as the charismatic corner commanded $4,716 through an energetic bidding war.


3. Deion Sanders 1989 Topps Traded #110T ($2,710)

Deion Sanders Rookie Card - Cincinnati Reds The base 1989 Topps Traded card features a young-looking Sanders in his Yankees cap and warm-ups, along with the more scarce Tiffany version that showcases a glossy finish. This card shows the lasting intrigue of Deion's baseball days and is a product that carries up to $2,710 as its top price.


4. 1989 Pro Set #486 Deion Sanders ($1,514)

An early Pro Set offering, this card of Sanders in his Florida State Seminoles gear shows why he was such a raw football talent. At $1,514 you may not be able to rush the field anymore like in college but just maybe this card can serve as a vivid homage to one of Sanders's many thrills he provided football fans and collectors.


5. Deion Sanders $4,320 (1993 Finest Refractors #141)

The most valuable card of his on the market today is likely the 1993 Finest Refractors #141 Deion Sanders PSA 10 (estimated value roughly $4,320) which should come as no surprise considering how successful it was. This card is one of the many pinnacles in Deion's career, further cementing its rank as a collector fan favorite.


Tracking Down These Treasures

Collectors can go on an exciting treasure hunt to locate these coveted cards by diving into many different online auction sites such as eBay, EBTH, Goldin, and numerous others. In addition, sports card shows and community forums as well as social media pages provide an element of fun ways to meet people who share your passion for collecting cards and discover treasures that you never knew existed. Delving into treasure hunting of Deion Sanders' most prized cards is an exciting path to take for people who are more interested in experiencing a bit of history.


In the End

In the field of sports card collecting, a chase for Deion Sanders' ultimate collectibles will forever bask in his immortal glory. Each of these cards tells a story, chapters in his illustrious career that can be found through the wonderful world as we walk along this enchanted universe and chart.
