Bella Hadid and The Weeknd's Cute Trip to Japan

Publish date: 2024-07-14

The coolest celebrity couple Bella Hadid and The Weeknd (whose real name is Abel Tesfaye) took a trip to Japan this week and shared some loved-up photos on Instagram.

The couple has been together since April 2015 and Tesfaye, 26, first opened up about dating the model in an interview with Rolling Stone in October 2015 saying they initially met through work: "I actually asked her to be on the artwork for Beauty Behind the Madness."

Since then the couple has made multiple red carpet appearances and now they are taking a break from it all to spend some quality time together in Tokyo, Japan. Tesfaye rarely posts photos with his 19-year-old girlfriend Bella, but he broke that trend with a mirror selfie on Instagram yesterday taken in Tokyo's Harajuku district.

The couple looked very chic, The Weeknd in a velvet jacket, jeans, leopard print sneakers and a surgical mask and Bella in a black jacket, tiny crop-top and navy camo pants.

Hadid recently opened to Glamour about their romance as well: "I'm dating Abel. I don't see him as The Weeknd," she said. "I'm proud of The Weeknd and the music he makes, but I really love Abel."
