Mistresses Star Rochelle Aytes Engaged to C.J. Lindsey: See Her Ring!

Publish date: 2024-09-02

April Malloy's getting married! Mistresses actress Rochelle Aytes is engaged to her longtime boyfriend C.J. Lindsey.

"He asked me to marry him and I said Yes! I feel so blessed to have this amazingly beautiful man in my life! God is so good. All the time!! @cj121075," Aytes, 39, captioned an Instagram pic of the couple and her new ring on Friday, July 17.

She also gave a shoutout to actress Kellee Stewart. "A special thanks to @kstew222 for initiating this union," Aytes gushed. "You are a beautiful soul!! #grateful."

An insider tells Us Weekly that the In the Morning actor popped the question to Aytes in the middle of a road trip from Vancouver to L.A. He pulled over and asked her on a beach.

"She was not expecting it all!" the source says. "It took her totally by surprise!"
