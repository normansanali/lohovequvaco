Emma Roberts News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-09-12

Name:Emma Roberts

Hometown:Rhinebeck, NY

Relationship:Single

Birthday:February 10, 1991

About

Despite her famous lineage (her aunt is Julia Roberts), the young actress — born Feb. 10, 1991 — has made quite the name for herself with a mature role in Blow (costarring Johnny Depp) and teen films such as Nancy Drew and Aquamarine.

In 2004, Emma Roberts hit the small screen starring in Nickelodeon’s Unfabulous, playing a seventh grade student who felt out of place. One year later, she lent her vocals to several songs on the show’s soundtrack and expressed interest in one day pursuing a music career.

The Neutrogena spokeswoman’s career is hotter than ever, with parts in the star-studded Valentine’s Day, the gritty drama Twelve (alongside Chace Crawford and Kiefer Sutherland) and the long-awaited Scream 4.

Roberts began dating Glee star Chord Overstreet in 2011. Her past loves include Alex Pettyfer and Dathan Kuppin. Roberts began dating Garrett Hedlund in March 2019 before welcoming son Rhodes in December 2020. Us confirmed their split in January 2022.
