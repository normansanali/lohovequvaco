Asa Hutchinson’s Education

He was born and raised by his parents in Bentonville, Arkansas, USA. Asa completed his schooling at Springdale High School. After completing his schooling, he got himself enrolled in the Bob Jones University in South Carolina in 1972 for a bachelor’s degree in accounting. Apart from this, he completed his law degree from the University of Arkansas School of Law in 1975. He started practicing law and, for almost 21 years, practiced in Fort Smith.
