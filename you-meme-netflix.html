Netflix's 'You' Has Spawned the Best Internet Memes

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Having just finished binging the fourth season of Netflix's 'You,' we can't think of a better way to digest than with memes. Keep scrolling.

Spoiler alert: These memes contain spoilers for Season 4 of Netflix's You.

If you came looking for memes about You, we probably don't have to tell you to run to Netflix to stream the Penn Badgley show, but it probably won't hurt to binge it again.

As a quick refresher, Season 4 of the delightfully dark series picks up following Joe Goldberg as he runs from the law in jolly old London. Joe is the most charming sociopathic killer we could imagine, which makes for some deliciously bingeworthy television.
