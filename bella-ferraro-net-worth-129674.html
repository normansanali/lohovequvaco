Bella Ferraro is an Australian singer who was born on August 20, 1994 in Sydney, Australia. She rose to fame after finishing in 4th place on the X Factor Australia in 2012. Following her X Factor tenure, she signed a contract with Sony Music Australia and released two charting singles, "Set Me on Fire" and "Forgot You". She later ended her contract with Sony.

About

Australian singer who finished in 4th place on the X Factor Australia in 2012. She signed a contract with Sony Music Australia and released charting singles "Set Me on Fire" and "Forgot You" after her X Factor tenure. She later ended her contract with Sony.

Before Fame

She began singing as a child and joined the Australian Girls Choir at the age of five. She began pursuing a career in music after leaving school in year 12.

Trivia

Covers that she performed on the X Factor found their way to Australian charts. Some of these covers include Counting Crows "Big Yellow Taxi," The Temper Trap's "Sweet Disposition" and One Direction's "What Makes You Beautiful."

Family Life

She was born and raised in Sydney, Australia.

Associated With

She cited Indie artists like Bon Iver, Lykke Li and Two Door Cinema Club as some of her musical influences.
