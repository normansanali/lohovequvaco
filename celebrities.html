Gary Oldman News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Name:Gary Oldman

Hometown:London, UK

Birthday:March 21, 1958

About

Known for his mastery of accents, English actor, writer, director, and producer Gary Oldman was born in 1958 and raised in London.

His most popular role was playing Count Dracula in Bram Stoker’s Dracula, but he has also received critical acclaim for roles in the Harry Potter film series, the Batman film series and for his performance as Sid Vicious in the 1986 Sid and Nancy.

Noted for his attempt to stay out of the Hollywood spotlight, the recovering alcoholic has been married three times to actress Lesley Manville, Uma Thurman, and model Donya Fiorentino. Oldman has three sons. He married his fourth wife, singer Alexandra Edenborough, in a secret ceremony in 2008.
