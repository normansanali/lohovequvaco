More Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic Matches Booked For 1/16 WWE NXT

Publish date: 2024-09-05

WWE brought a live episode of NXT for fans on January 9th, and they were able to set the stage for next week’s episode.

Ringside News provided comprehensive play-by-play coverage of WWE NXT this week. It was quite a show, complete with two title matches.

During this week’s episode of WWE NXT, they kicked off the opening round for the 2024 Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic. Next week’s show will see two more matches, as four teams battle in the tournament.

The WWE NXT card for January 16th includes a Women’s #1 Contender Battle Royal Match, as well as a Dusty Cup Tournament Match featuring Carmelo Hayes and Trick Williams against Edris Enofe and Malik Blade. Another Dusty Cup Tournament Match is set between LWO and Chase U.

That women’s battle royal match will be an exciting addition to the show. The final four competitors will then compete in a fatal four-way match, where the winner will face Lyra Valkyria for her NXT Women’s Title.

We will have full coverage of WWE NXT, and the rest of the pro wrestling world, here at Ringside News. You never know what will break next, but we will be here with all the updates you need.

WWE NXT 1/16 Card:

  • Women’s #1 Contender Battle Royal Match
  • Carmelo Hayes & Trick Williams vs Edris Enofe & Malik Blade – Dusty Cup Tournament Match
  • LWO vs Chase U – Dusty Cup Tournament Match
  • Lyra Valkyria & Tatum Paxley vs Lola Vice & Elektra Lopez
