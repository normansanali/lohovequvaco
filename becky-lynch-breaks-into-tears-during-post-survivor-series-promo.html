Becky Lynch Breaks Into Tears During Post Survivor Series Promo

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Becky Lynch kicked off Survivor Series by defeating Charlotte Flair. After the match was over, Lynch cut a promo backstage and she got very emotional in the process.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE Survivor Series

Lynch cut a promo backstage and she was crying while speaking about the hatred that they walked to the ring with at Survivor Series. Both Lynch and Flair came a long way and those two gave it their all in Brooklyn.

The Man cried while talking about everything that she’s been through with Flair. They trained together, they almost died in a car crash together, and Lynch trusted Flair with her life.

Mick Foley saw the promo and he was moved by The Man’s words. “Good on you @BeckyLynchWWE Our business is built on great rivalries, but it’s tough to make magic with someone you truly despise,” Mick tweeted out.

This was a pretty emotional promo and not what you would expect in a post-Survivor Series victory celebration. You can check out the video below.
