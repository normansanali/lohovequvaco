Paige DeSorbo and Craig Conovers Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Paige DeSorbo and Craig Conover were friends for years before their relationship took a romantic turn.

The Southern Charm star and the Summer House personality crossed paths while starring on their respective Bravo shows. “I met Craig, actually, three years ago and he was single. I had a boyfriend, and I absolutely respected that I was in a relationship,” DeSorbo exclusively told Us Weekly in October 2021, referring to ex Perry Rahbar.

While they initially struck up a friendship, DeSorbo and Conover spent more time together in 2021 when they filmed Winter House — but never crossed the line. Conover was dating Natalie Hegnauer at the time, but the pair called it quits not long after he wrapped the crossover series.

Conover confirmed he was single in May 2021. Things between him and DeSorbo were on by the summer, per insiders, and by the fall, the twosome were officially dating.
