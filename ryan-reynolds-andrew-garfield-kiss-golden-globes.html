Ryan Reynolds and Andrew Garfield Kiss at the Golden Globes

Publish date: 2024-09-17

While you were busy being dazzled by Ryan Gosling winning the award for Best Actor in a movie, you probably missed a crucial Golden Globes moment: Ryan Reynolds and Andrew Garfield sharing a steamy kiss in the audience.

It all happened so fast, but while Gosling was making his way to the stage, Reynolds and Garfield celebrated his win by kissing at their table (all wins should be celebrated like this).

Nobody thought this was more hilarious than Blake Lively, of course, who sat next to Reynolds laughing and giving them a round of applause.
