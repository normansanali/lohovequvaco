Charlotte Flair Hits Back At Hater Who Called Her Out For Getting Too Much Plastic Surgery

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Charlotte Flair has been a regular fixture of WWE television for almost a decade now and continues to pave her way in the company. She remains a highly divisive figure, as most fans don’t want her near the main event scene. That said, it appears Flair hit back at a hater who called her out for getting too much plastic surgery.

The Queen of WWE returned to WWE television last month and has been in hot pursuit of Asuka’s WWE Women’s World Championship. Naturally, not everyone is happy about this whatsoever.

That said, Charlotte Flair has been posting photos of herself on social media, where she showed off her physique. Her latest post on Instagram featured her wearing a bikini at the beach.

While most fans complimented her, one particular hater called her out for getting too much plastic surgery. Charlotte Flair didn’t fail to notice the abrasive comment and hit back at the hater.

Charlotte Flair will likely square off against Bianca Belair and Asuka in a Triple Threat match for the title at SummerSlam this year. We will have to wait and see how their feud will be booked till then.
