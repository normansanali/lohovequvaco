Carole Middleton's Mother-of-the-Bride Style

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Media Platforms Design Team

"Mother of the bride" doesn't have to mean dowdy, as Kate's stunning mom proves. At left, Carole Middleton shows off a Catherine Walker wool crepe coatdress and silk shantung day dress. (The late Walker was also a favorite designer of Princess Diana's.) Her hat is by milliner Berkshire-based Jane Corbett. Talk about fabulous at every age!
