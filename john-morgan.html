Biography

John Morgan was born in Dallas, TX on March 15, 1978. A journalist who is best known for his coverage of a variety of MMA events. He is also a radio host and television commentator for the sport as well. He was nominated for the 2010 and 2011 Journalist of the Year at the World MMA Awards along with fellow journalist Ariel Helwani. With a passion for the sport at a young age, he began covering the sport in 2007 where he became the lead staff reporter and co-owner of the online magazine MMA Junkie.
