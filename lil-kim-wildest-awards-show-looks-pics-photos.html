Pics From Grammys &amp; More Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Lil Kim is a legend and pioneer for women in rap. And she’s also one of the most iconic awards show attendees of all time. Not for her crazy antics; it’s her wild outfits! Kim is teensy tiny, but she manages to make herself larger than life when she hits the red carpet at an awards show, whether it’s the Grammy Awards, the MTV Video Music Awards, the BET Hip-Hop Awards, or anything in between. She has fun with her outfits, and never shies away from showing some skin while showcasing her sexy style. Take her now-infamous outfit that she rocked at the 1999 Grammys. Kim rolled up wearing a jumpsuit designed by her personal stylist that made her look like a mermaid. The lilac jumpsuit was one-shouldered, and covered in sparkly seashell appliqués.
