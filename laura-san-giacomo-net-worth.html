Laura San Giacomo is an American actress. She had her career beginnings in the 1980s, first appearing in an episode of Spenser: For Hire. In 1989, she starred as Cynthia Patrice Bishop in the film Sex, Lies, and Videotape. Her performance here earned her Golden Globe and BAFTA nominations for Best Supporting Actress. The following decade, she had roles in the films Pretty Woman, Under Suspicion, Nina Takes a Lover, and Suicide Kings. She also rose to fame for her role as Maya Galloin the NBC sitcom Just Shoot Me! In the 2000s, she also became known for starring as Rhetta Rodriguez in the TNT series Saving Grace. She currently has recurring roles in NCIS as Dr. Grace Confalone and Animal Kingdom Morgan Wilson.
