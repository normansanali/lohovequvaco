Jimmy Butler’s Career Highlights

Butler’s early years with the Bulls witnessed steady improvement as he worked diligently to hone his skills and establish himself as a critical player for the team. In 2015, he secured a lucrative five-year, $95 million contract with the Bulls, solidifying his position as a valuable asset in the league.

Over the years, Butler’s determination and tenacity have led to outstanding performances across various teams.
