Biography

Macarena Achaga Figueroa (born March 5, 1992) is a multi-talented Argentinian starlet renowned for her work as an actress, model, singer, and television host. Hailing from Argentina, she has mesmerized audiences worldwide with her diverse skillset. 

Initially rising to prominence as a member of the Mexcian-Argentinian pop group Eme 15, she has since made a name for herself as an accomplished actress, model, singer, and television hostess.

Debuting as an actress in 2012 with her role in the Mexican television series Miss XV, Macarena Achaga captivated audiences with her natural acting talent and undeniable screen presence. Her performance quickly garnered attention, propelling her into the limelight and marking the beginning of her illustrious career.

Throughout the years, Macarena Achaga‘s impressive body of work has solidified her reputation as a true force to be reckoned with in the entertainment industry. However, Macarena Achaga‘s unforgettable portrayal of Valentina Carvajal in the hit television series Love to Death catapulted her to new heights of fame and cemented her as a household name in Mexico and Argentina.

Early Life

Macarena Achaga was born on March 5th, 1992, in the picturesque city of Mar del Plata in Argentina. Her parents, both architects, raised her and her younger brother Santiago Achaga, who arrived eight years after her. Her mother, Soledad Figueroa, and father, Leopoldo Achaga, instilled in her an appreciation for creativity and hard work from a young age.

At age fifteen, Macarena Achaga took a bold step towards pursuing her career as a model by travelling five hours weekly by bus to Buenos Aires. She quickly caught the attention of Pink Model Management and Multitalent Agency, leading to her deciding to move to the city to further her modelling dreams when she turned eighteen.

While the fast-paced lifestyle of Buenos Aires initially proved challenging for her, Macarena Achaga persevered and emerged as one of the most accomplished models in Argentina, with a host of successful campaigns and high-profile events under her belt.

Personal Life

Macarena Achaga‘s life has been a rollercoaster ride of events since she made her way to Mexico City to play a lead role in Miss XV. Her transformation into a vegetarian in 2013 marked a significant change in her lifestyle, which she attributed to her growing concern for animal welfare.

Despite the breakup with her former partner Sebastian Zurita at the end of 2014, the actress didn’t let it dampen her spirits and focused on her career. In 2021, Macarena Achaga went public about her romantic relationship with Juanpa Zurita, whom she met while filming Luis Miguel: La Serie 2, and their social media posts were enough to set the internet ablaze.

It’s also worth mentioning that the talented actress once dated Bárbara López, which was another much-talked-about event in her personal life.

Career

Macarena Achaga, the Argentine beauty, kickstarted her modelling career at 15 with Pink Models Management in Buenos Aires, her hometown. Her first modelling gigs were for Sweet Victorians, Doll Fins, and Muaaa, all big names in the Argentine fashion industry. Later, she signed up with Multitalent Agency, a top modelling agency in Argentina, and became a popular face in many print and commercial campaigns.

Her captivating looks and modelling talent landed her gigs with prominent brands such as Marinela Cookies and CLOĒ handbags, for which she appeared in their print campaigns in 2010 and 2012, respectively. Additionally, she graced the covers of ParaTeens, Código Teen, Joy, Para Ti, and Hola magazines, which made her a household name in Argentina and beyond.

After establishing herself as a successful model in Argentina, Macarena Achaga took her talent overseas, briefly working in Mexico and France before landing her first TV gig as co-host of the Argentine version of MTV Latin America’s musical show Los 10+ Pedidos in 2010. She left the show the following year after securing a role in Nickelodeon Latin America and Canal 5’s TV series Miss XV.

Macarena Achaga‘s acting talent shone through in Miss XV, which led her to audition for the antagonist role of Leonora Martínez in the Mexican TV show. She moved to Mexico City to attend workshops at Televisa San Ángel and landed the role, which garnered critical acclaim and cemented her status as a talented actress.

To expand her range as an actress, Macarena Achaga took on the challenge of playing a Mexican character in the Mexican remake of Gossip Girl, Gossip Girl: Acapulco. To prepare for the role, she took speech lessons to eliminate her Argentine accent and accurately portray the character.

In addition to her TV roles, Macarena Achaga has appeared in several films, including the Peruvian drama film Magallanes and the Chilean drama Sitiados. She also starred as the protagonist in the short film El Librito Rojo, which was filmed in Guanajuato, Mexico.

In 2011, Macarena Achaga joined the Mexican reality show Miss XV cast and became the sixth and final member of the pop group Eme 15, alongside Paulina Goto, Elezar Gomez, Natasha Dupeyron, Yago Muñoz, and Jack Duarte. The group released their debut music video, Wonderland, in April 2012 and performed together until their final concert in January 2014.
