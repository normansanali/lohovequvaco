Amal Clooney Is Dressed Like Royalty During Latest Date Night with George

Publish date: 2024-09-12

Amal Clooney is constantly proving that her eveningwear game is unmatched.

While attending the Academy Museum's annual gala in Los Angeles this past weekend, the human rights lawyer looked like actual royalty in a flowing, ruched gown from Milan-based brand Del Core's 2023 resort collection.

The elegant dress featured ruffled, fluid panels of lime and olive green and creamy white hues. The strapless sweetheart neckline additionally included a diamond-shaped keyhole cutout in the center of the bustier.

Amal accessorized with diamond waterfall earrings, silver bracelets, a green box clutch, and silver metallic heels.

Meanwhile, husband George Clooney accompanied her in a classic black-and-white tux.

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

Amy Sussman//Getty Images

Amal is known for pulling out all the stops when it comes to her sophisticated date night style.

While attending last month's world premiere of George's new rom-com, Ticket to Paradise, Amal channeled the Art Deco era in an icy pale blue gown with sequins arranged in a classic chevron pattern. She paired the luminescent slipdress with a pair of champagne-colored pumps and further accessorized with a metallic rectangular clutch, diamond chandelier earrings, and a floral diamond bracelet.

She looked equally stunning at the movie premiere's after-party, where she wore a sequined yellow minidress with a mock turtleneck and flared long sleeves. She finished the vibrant ensemble with a pair of reflective silver pointed-toe slingback pumps and a matching clutch. For jewelry, Amal had opted for a pair of large diamond stud earrings.
