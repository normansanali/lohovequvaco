New Suicide Squad Still Highlights Harley Quinn And Deadshot

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Recommended Videos

Eager to not let Captain America: Civil War steal too much of the spotlight as the film approaches its release, Warner Bros. is continuing to pump material out of the Suicide Squad marketing machine.

After giving us an awesome new trailer a few weeks back, the studio now has a new still for us to feast our eyes on – and you can check it out for yourself above.

As you can see, the image puts the spotlight on Deadshot (Will Smith) and Harley Quinn (Margot Robbie), who will be involved in some sort of “ragged romantic triangle” along with The Joker, according to Smith. They look quite infatuated with each other in the photo above, and it will be interesting to see how this romance eventually plays out in the film.

Suicide Squad bursts into theatres on August 5th. Tell us, are you looking forward to seeing Deadshot and Harley in action? Sound off with your thoughts in the comments section below!
