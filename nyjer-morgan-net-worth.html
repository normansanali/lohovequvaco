Nyjer Morgan’s Career and Controversies

Nyjer Morgan, also known as “Tony Plush,” is a Major League Baseball outfielder who has played for three teams: The Pittsburgh Pirates, the Washington Nationals, and the Milwaukee Brewers. He has a net worth of $1 million.

Morgan gained attention for his catch on September 14th, 2007, which sent the Pirates to a 4 to 3 victory against the Brewers. Morgan’s star in baseball has continued to rise, but he hasn’t been free from controversy. On May 23rd, 2010, Morgan reacted angrily to a missed ball by tossing his glove to the side and storming off the field. He has also courted controversy through his Twitter account, which he often uses to taunt his fellow players.

Additionally, Morgan’s former girlfriend Jordana Lenz appeared as a cast member on the VH1 reality show Baseball Wives in 2011. She has been described by fans and viewers of the show as rude, flirtatious, and a gold digger. Morgan and Lenz dated for a little over a year.
