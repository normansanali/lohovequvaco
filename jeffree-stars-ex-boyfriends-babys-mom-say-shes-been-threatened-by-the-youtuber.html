Jeffree Star's Ex Boyfriend's Baby's Mom Say She's Been 'Threatened' By The YouTuber

Publish date: 2024-08-03

When Jeffree Star shared a picture cradling a heavily tattooed man - no one was more shocked than the mystery man's baby's mother.

The now ex boyfriend of Jeffree Star - Andre Marhold - has been accused of stealing from the YouTuber.

Marhold hit back - claiming Star paid him to date him and has the AIDS virus - something which Star has denied.

Shortly after the beauty guru went public with his romance last month, Marhold's baby mom - Emmanuella Chartol - took to Instagram to express her devastation.

The French model shared a photo of the basketball player and their son playing in the water.

"My son matters. My son was so happy last week. Idc if it's about money but this hurts me a lot and affects my son." She continued, "God please help me to keep my head up for my son cause this s**t is unreal. Please help me God to be strong for my son."

Emmanuella later shared a photo of her and Andre together along with the caption that read, "Tell me what I've done to you? Tell me why to expose yourself but my son? Money? Cause I could work harder to make our life better. I was your ride and die, I could do anything to save our relationship. Anything."

Then just a few hours ago, Emanuella shared a heartbreaking message, where you could he crying in the background.

Chartol wrote: "Everyday is a nightmare to live with this BS. I barely sleep, I lost my focus. I don't like going out anymore. I'm trying to be strong for my son who deserves my full attention as mother and father."

Shockingly, she then goes on to say Jeffree Star has been "harassing" her.

"Being humiliated in public. I never deserved it. I've been attacked/ harassed in my DM FROM JF. (I've never talked to him on twitter or Instagram) cause this guy was looking for the father of my child. The disrespect is real."
